About Me

I am a third-year student of Business Information Technology at JAMK, specializing in game development. I have a keen interest in game programming and gameplay programming, and I possess strong skills in Unity and C#. I am also currently expanding my knowledge of Unreal Engine 5. As a part of my studies, I have been involved in several school projects, where I have gained valuable experience in project management and teamwork. My latest project, 13 Candles, was published on Steam, where I served as lead programmer and product owner. My current focus is on my thesis, which explores the topic of Gamification. I have a strong passion for this area of study and intend to continue learning and exploring this field beyond graduation. Overall, my experience and education have equipped me with the skills and knowledge necessary to excel in the field of game development, particularly in the areas of game programming and gamification.
